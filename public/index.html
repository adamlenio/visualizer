From the studio

We got tired of watching good lighting get value-engineered away.

For three decades I've helped lighting and smart-technology brands tell their stories. In that time I've watched the same thing happen on project after project: a thoughtful lighting scheme gets value-engineered away, because the client can't picture what they're paying for. A rendering on a page doesn't let you feel the difference between 2700K and 4000K, or what a scene does to a room at dusk.

So I built the thing I wished existed: step inside the design and touch it, using the exact controls that will run the space once it's installed. The Office is where we started. It isn't where we intend to stop.

— Adam Lenio, founder, AD&M
